Bisho High School

Bisho High School Was established in January 2006.The school is situated outside King William’s Town.
This school of excellence has grown ever since, having its own matriculants in 2011.
This school has a very diverse learner population, with learners from all walks of life, race, greed, and culture.
Our school is a relatively small school, learners are able to enjoy classes, and the personal attention on a day-to-day basis from the educators.
Our School employs a team of qualified, experienced and committed staff members that always go beyond the call of duty to enthuse the learners with the best education,
sport and culture possible.
We strive to be committed to the students we are privileged to serve, in our alumni association, our dedicated, talented faculty and staff.
We also take pride in having the most active and helpful group of partners, visionary parents, well-wishers, alumni and friends.

Mission Statement

Bisho High School strives to provide quality education within a caring environment that balances
the pursuit of academic excellence and the development of life skills,
so as to empower all of the learners to achieve their potential and meet the challenges of a dynamic society as responsible citizens.

Our Core Values

We have a culture that is modern, relevant, and inspires students to have a brighter future.
We are determined in our approach to learning, are creative in our thinking, and bold in our ambitions.

Vision

It is the school’s vision that each matriculant leaving Bisho High School will leave with a sound academic foundation,
will have participated actively in various spheres of the school, will have learnt morals,
respect and represent all that is noble and good within the community of King Williams Town.
Each matriculant will wear their uniform with pride and upon leaving the school their achievements will enable us to wish them well for their bright futures.
